About

About Us - Chabad of White Plains

Welcome to Chabad of White Plains! We are a vibrant and inclusive center for Jewish life in White Plains, dedicated to serving the local Jewish community and beyond. Guided by the leadership of Rabbi Zalmy and Musia Plotke, we have proudly been part of White Plains since 2021, creating a warm and inviting space where everyone can explore and celebrate Jewish culture, heritage, and values.

Our Vision

Our vision is firmly rooted in the love for humanity and the core principles of Chabad-Lubavitch philosophy. We place a strong emphasis on cherishing every member of the Jewish community and reaching out to each individual. Our mission is to enrich Jewish life in White Plains by offering educational, religious, social, and cultural activities for people of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Activities

Chabad of White Plains offers a diverse range of programs and events, including engaging Torah classes, festive holiday celebrations, lively Shabbat meals, and enriching youth programs. Our primary goal is to provide a welcoming, non-judgmental environment where everyone can learn, grow, and experience the joys of Judaism.

Join Us

Whether you're new to White Plains, just visiting, or have been a part of this community for years, we wholeheartedly welcome you to be a part of our Chabad family. At Chabad, there are no membership fees or tickets required – everyone is considered family. We invite you to explore our website, participate in our programs, and become an integral part of our vibrant community!
